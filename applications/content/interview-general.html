Guidelines
  1. Do an initial greeting! Give a strong, friendly first impression, introduce yourself and thank them for their interest in Void Gaming.
  2. Find the application. If there is not one, guide them through the application process.
  3. Make sure the application has been accepted before you proceed. If not approved, go through the things they need in the app: 16+, working microphone, and have read the Code of Conduct.
  4. Ask if they are familiar with Discord. If not, teach them the basics (enable mic, mute sound, voice settings, move voice channels, PM, text channels, account settings, desktop app etc.).
  5. Explain the basis of a trial. It's a 7 day period, where members will be able to upvote/downvote them in regards to friendliness, positivity, and activity. It'll be on a thread called a TFT which will be private from other trialists and from himself. It's to prevent negative players from joining. They are not bound to their initial section.
