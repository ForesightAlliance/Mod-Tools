Guidelines
  1. Do an initial greeting! Give a strong, friendly first impression, introduce yourself and thank them for their interest in Ethereal Gaming.
  2. Find the application. If there is not one, guide them through the application process.
  3. Make sure the application has been accepted before you proceed. If not approved, go through the things they need in the app: 16+, working microphone, and have read the Code of Conduct.
  4. Ask if they are familiar with TS. If not, teach them the basics (enable mic, mute sound, set away, move channels, PM, and poke).
  5. Explain the basis of a trial. It's 2 a 2 week period, where members will be able to upvote/downvote them in regards to friendliness, positivity, and activity. It'll be on a thread called a TED which will be private from other trialists and from himself. It's to prevent negative players from joining. They are not bound to their initial section.
  6. Create a TED. (Link to Instructions)
  7. Change the Application’s Prefix to Trial (Link to Instructions)
  8. Comment on the application using the Application: On Trial line on the application tool.
  9. Add the link to the TED to the trialist’s description on Teamspeak (Right click on client - Change description) and give them Teamspeak Trial permissions.
  10. Explain any other relevant information for the specific game (teams, events, chatrooms, etc.)
  11. Change the forum usergroup from Guest to Trialist. (ou can find instructions here, but the basis is go to their forum profile, click the little pencil above their name, log in, change the primary usergroup to "Trial".)
  12. Remind and encourage the new Trialist to be active on both TS and the Forums, as that is the activity we are looking for at the moment.
  13. Speak about how we handle incidents. Link them the Who do I contact? thread. Tell them to reach a moderator if anything negative happens, no matter how petty. If they find it offensive, let them know staff is here to help.
