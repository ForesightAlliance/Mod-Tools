Guidelines
  1. Do an initial greeting! Give a strong, friendly first impression, introduce yourself and thank them for their interest in Ethereal Gaming.
  2. Find the application. (If he/she has not made one yet, ask them to).
  3. Make sure the application has been accepted before you proceed.
  4. Create a TED. (Link to Instructions)
  5. Change the Application’s Prefix to Trial (Link to Instructions)
  6. Comment on the application using the Application: On Trial line on the application tool.
  7. Add the link to the TED to the trialist’s description on Teamspeak (Right click on client - Change description) and give them Teamspeak Trial permissions.
  8. Explain the basis of a trial (How a TED works, what it is, forum/ts/game activity, etc)
  9. Explain any other relevant information for the specific game (teams, events, chatrooms, etc.)
  10. Change the forum usergroup from Guest to Trialist. (ou can find instructions here, but the basis is go to their forum profile, click the little pencil above their name, log in, change the primary usergroup to "Trial".)
  11. Remind and encourage the new Trialist to be active on both TS and the Forums, as that is the activity we are looking for at the moment.
